About me

For those who would like to know more about me, here is a short summary: I was born and raised in France, in Haute Savoie, in a village located in the middle of a ski area that is now called Les Portes du Soleil. And yes, you may have guessed it but I love skiing, thanks to my father. My mother, the last born of a family of stone masons, and therefore a draughtsman, "transmitted" the virus of drawing to me, except that at my time, we drew on a computer. I first obtained a CAP (vocational training certificate) in carpentry and fittings but with a lot of experience in carpentry and roofing. Then I got a title of building electrician and finally a CAP in wood frame construction. I am now in professional reconvention in the web development where my goal is to help small companies to be known. You will have understood, I have a lot of knowledge in the field of the building "wood". I spent part of my life abroad, especially in the Anglo-Saxon countries.
